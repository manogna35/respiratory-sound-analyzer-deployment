AI-Powered Respiratory Health Sound Analyzer

This intelligent healthcare system analyzes cough, breathing, wheezing, and crackle sounds to support early detection of respiratory diseases using Artificial Intelligence and advanced audio signal processing. The system converts respiratory audio into spectrograms and extracts meaningful acoustic features to classify normal and abnormal patterns with high efficiency. It is designed to support hospitals, clinics, telemedicine platforms, and personal health monitoring.
